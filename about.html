About Me

Hi! I'm Chelsea, a UX/UI Designer and Web Developer with a unique background in Outdoor Education and Adventure Tourism. My extensive experience working with participants of all ages in educational and adventure-based environments has shaped my ability to empathize and connect with people in meaningful ways. Whether navigating challenges in the backcountry or guiding students through transformative experiences, I’ve cultivated a solution-oriented mindset that underpins my work as a designer and developer. My past roles have equipped me with the resilience, creativity, and initiative to identify potential issues and address them proactively. Educators are inventive professionals who always put the needs of others first—a perspective I carry into my designs and code. I strive to produce user-focused, high-quality solutions with precision and thoughtfulness, ensuring an impactful and intuitive user experience. Flexibility and adaptability, honed through years of outdoor education, have been invaluable in my design and development career. Embracing change with ease has allowed me to navigate transitions seamlessly, whether tackling new challenges or learning new technologies. When I'm not immersed in crafting engaging digital experiences, you can find me exploring the great outdoors with my husband and dog, reading a captivating book, or enjoying time with friends and family.
