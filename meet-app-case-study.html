ACEP Trailblazers Luncheon

Exclusive Gathering

Congratulations ACEP Trailblazers,

Despite my 54 years in ACEP, I don't know all of you personally - the recipients of ACEP's Trailblazer award. Each of you has made remarkable contributions to emergency medicine, and I would like to remedy this gap by bringing us all together.

I'm personally inviting you to join me for an intimate lunch where we can share stories, learn from each other's experiences, and strengthen the bonds within our Trailblazer community.
